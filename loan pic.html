Why CARS24?

  • 12-Month Warranty
  • 7-Day Return
  • Easy Financing Options
  • 140-Quality Checks

Buying a car is always an exciting decision, but we understand that sometimes things don’t work out. We ensure every car we sell goes through a thorough inspection, refurbishment and service, but you may still feel like the car you bought is not for you. Although we hope that never happens, we offer easy 7-day returns (as long as the car has run less than 500 km) in case the car is not right for you. No questions asked.

Here’s how it works:

  • Once you buy a car, you can test it for 7 days at leisure. See if it meets your requirements, is functioning properly and most of all, if it’s the right car for you. Drive on roads you typically would to test the suspension, play your favourite music; treat it as your car
  • If you aren’t happy with the car, you can write to us at support@carioauto.com within 7 days of delivery
  • Our team will inspect the car to ensure it is in the same condition as when we sold it to you
  • Once details are verified, the car will be picked up and a refund will be issued within 2 working days

We encourage you to test your new vehicle thoroughly (but carefully!) during this period. Take it on the routes or surfaces you intend to use it for. Get your family in. Test everything from the suspension to the sound system. It’s all yours!

Terms & Conditions for the CARS24 7-day easy return

  • A return request must be raised within 7 days of car delivery
  • If the car has met with an accident, is modified, altered, or damaged, the return policy will not be valid. The vehicle should be in the same condition it was delivered in, so please take care!
  • The car should not have been driven more than 500 km from the date of delivery

Frequently asked questions

When will my payment be processed if I return a vehicle under the 7-day trial?

The payment will be processed within 2 working days from the accepted date of return once the inspection of the car is completed and everything is as it was before. 

Can I buy another car after I return the car under the 7 day return policy?

Yes, once your return has been processed, you can choose to buy another car from us - and we would love to deliver it to you! However, you may only return 3 cars for a complete refund during this trial in order to be fair to our community.

What will the refund payment method be?

Regardless of how you paid at the time of purchase, all refunds are processed online. If you made your payment online, your original method of payment will get the refund.

What if there’s an accessory missing at the time of refund or pick-up?

If there’s any change in the vehicle’s condition at the time, or an accessory missing with respect to the checklist you signed during purchase, that amount will be deducted and the rest of the amount will be refunded. This amount will be decided by our technical team.

What if I face an issue with my car after the 7 day return period?

Rest assured, we’ve taken utmost care to make sure that this will not be the case - each car listed on the platform goes through rigorous testing and inspection. In any case, you don’t need to worry - each car comes with a 12-month warranty so you can get it repaired for free from our authorized partners.
